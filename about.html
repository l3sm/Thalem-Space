About Thalem Space

Thalem Space is a collection of simple, open‑source tools designed to help freelancers, indie makers and small teams work smarter. Each tool solves a specific problem, stays out of your way and respects your data.

Why open source? Because transparency builds trust. Our code is available for anyone to inspect, audit or improve. Purchases help support continued development and keep these projects alive.

Why pay? Your contributions fund new features, bug fixes and the maintenance required to keep tools useful in the long term. You also get lifetime updates via our public releases, so you always have the latest version.

No accounts needed. Tools run locally in your browser or on your operating system. When you buy a tool, you receive a link to its releases page where you can always download the latest version.
